# Separable

Group of attributes to separate information/elements.
Connected to Container

- Level 1 - used when subtle division is needed/to divide similar items e.g. options within a list, individual comments
- Level 2 - used to divide related areas e.g. heading and body text
- Level 3 - used to separate areas of content that are not closely related e.g. app header from main content
