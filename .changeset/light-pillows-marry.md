Add `CardHeader`, `CardContent` and `CardFooter`, composable layout sections that own the inner padding of a card. Use them as direct children of `Card`, `LinkCard` or `InteractableCard` to create structured layouts and allow other children, such as images or dividers, to extend to the card's edges. When a section is present, the wrapping card's default padding is automatically removed.
