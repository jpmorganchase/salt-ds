Added `SidePanel`.

`SidePanel` is a collapsible container that slides in from an edge of its parent, providing supplementary content or controls without disrupting the main layout.

```tsx
<SidePanelGroup>
  <SidePanelTrigger />
  <SidePanel>
    <SidePanelCloseButton />
  </SidePanel>
</SidePanelGroup>
```
