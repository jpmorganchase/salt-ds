### Keyboard interactions

<KeyboardControls>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Enter / Space">
    - When focus is on Pill, it is activated.
  </KeyboardControl>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Tab">
    - Moves focus into Pill. Focus is set to the entire Pill. - When Pill has
    focus, move focus out of Pill to next component in tab order.
  </KeyboardControl>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Tab + Shift">
    - When Pill has focus, move focus out of Pill to previous component in tab
    order.
  </KeyboardControl>
</KeyboardControls>
