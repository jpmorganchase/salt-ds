## Keyboard interactions

### Pill

<KeyboardControls>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Enter / Space">
    When focus is on the pill, this action activates it.
  </KeyboardControl>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Tab">

    - Tab sets focus on the entire pill.
    - When the pill has focus, Tab moves focus out of the pill to next component in tab order.

  </KeyboardControl>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Shift + Tab">
    When the pill has focus, this action moves focus out of the pill to the previous component in the tab
    order.
  </KeyboardControl>
</KeyboardControls>

### Selectable pill group

<KeyboardControls>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Space">
    When focus is on the pill within a selectable group, this action toggles its
    selection state (selects or deselects the pill).
  </KeyboardControl>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Tab">
    When focus is before the pill group, this action moves focus to the first
    pill in the group.
  </KeyboardControl>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Shift + Tab">
    When focus is after the pill group, this action will shift focus to the last
    pill in the group.
  </KeyboardControl>
</KeyboardControls>
