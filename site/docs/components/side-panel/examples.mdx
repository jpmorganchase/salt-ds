## Default

By default, a side panel anchors to the `left` edge of the screen.

You can place side panel on the `left`, `right`, `top` and `bottom` edges using the `side` prop.

<LivePreview componentName="side-panel" exampleName="LeftPanel" />

## Right side panel

Consider using a right-anchored side panel to show supporting information related to the current context, such as recent events, notifications or quick settings. Always set your side panel's width suitable for the content inside it.

<LivePreview componentName="side-panel" exampleName="RightPanel" />

## Top side panel

Anchor your side panel to the top edge if it will display supporting information that’s related to the current context and is best presented in a horizontal format, e.g., complex filters or a summary panel.

<LivePreview componentName="side-panel" exampleName="TopPanel" />

## Bottom side panel

Anchor a side panel to the bottom edge of the screen when using a master-detail layout with content, such as graphs, images or comparisons that requires a wider horizontal format.

<LivePreview componentName="side-panel" exampleName="BottomPanel" />
