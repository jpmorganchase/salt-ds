<Callout title="A11y Note">
  <div>
  Ensure the toggletip's trigger button has a meaningful accessible name so screen reader users understand what it relates to.
  </div>
</Callout>

## Default

The toggletip consists of a trigger and panel. The panel is revealed on click of the trigger and remains persistent until the user dismisses it.

<LivePreview componentName="toggletip" exampleName="Default" />

## Placement

Use the `placement` prop to specify the position of the panel relative to the trigger. Choose the placement that obscures the least amount of content and critical interface elements. The default placement is `top`.

<LivePreview componentName="toggletip" exampleName="Placement" />

## Long content

In most cases, scrolling shouldn't be required. However, if you have a large amount of content that can't be avoided, the panel will scroll internally.
